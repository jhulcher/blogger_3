TRANSPARENCY MEETS SUPPLY CHAIN
THE NETWORK SCORE℠

"If your product could say what it needs to be healthy, what would it say?"

The Paradata Network Score measures a supply chain in four dimensions: Leadtime, Cost, Compliance, and Risk.
LEADTIME

"Which components will prevent me from shipping product?"

Leadtime scoring evaluates and simulates the impact of component decisions before committing... that’s transparency.
COST

"Are my component prices market competitive?"

" What should I be paying based on future demand?"

Cost scoring measures the variance between supplier-provided costs vs the best available market cost... that’s transparency.
COMPLIANCE

"Are all my products compliant to current regulations?"

"Am I prepared for new regulations?"

Compliance scoring measures the ability of product data to support rigorous examination of import/export compliance... that’s transparency.
RISK

"What is my sole source component status by product?"

"Do I have any components going end of life? If yes, which ones?"

Risk scoring measures how a component is sourced, proximity to manufacturing locations, supplier performance... that’s transparency.
